Network Security

At TeamFence, we specialize in providing actionable insights through Network Assessment services. Our approach involves simulating a diverse range of cyber-attacks to assess the security of your network or IT system comprehensively. Our ultimate goal is to furnish you with clear and digestible results that spotlight both the vulnerabilities and strengths of your system, empowering you to make informed decisions to bolster your digital assets effectively.

External Network Penetration Test (eNPT)

An External Network Penetration Test (eNPT) is a specialized assessment conducted from outside the company's network, simulating the actions of an external malicious actor. This test, focuses on evaluating the security of your external-facing systems and infrastructure, such as web servers, applications, and network perimeter devices.

Internal Network Penetration Test (iNPT)

An Internal Network Penetration Test (iNPT) is a specialized assessment conducted from within the company's network, emulating the actions of an internal malicious actor, such as a consultant or an employee. This test, provides insights into how vulnerable your network may be to insider threats or unauthorized access from within the organization.

Active Directory Assessment (ADA)

During an Active Directory Assessment, our team thoroughly examines your Active Directory configuration, policies, permissions, group memberships, trust relationships, and overall security posture. We utilize industry best practices and specialized tools to identify potential misconfigurations, vulnerabilities, and security weaknesses.
